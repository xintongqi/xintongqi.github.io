C Programming

Most useful advice I ever read for deciphering C types:
"Start at the name, read right when you can, left when you must"
char (*a[2])[14] - start at a, move right: "array of two", move left: "pointer to", bracket complete so read right: "array of forteen", read left: "char"...
Put it together and we have "a is array of two pointers to arrays of forteen chars"
